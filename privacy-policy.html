Privacy Policy

Updated: 02/10/2024

At Aupulens, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you engage with our services, purchase our finance related PDFs, or visit our website. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information: When you interact with Aupulens, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, and billing details. This information is collected when you sign up for our services, purchase a product, or contact us for support.
Usage Data: We automatically gather certain information when you visit our website, such as your IP address, browser type, pages viewed, and how long you spend on each page. This data helps us improve the overall user experience and optimize our content.
Cookies: Our website uses cookies to store small amounts of data on your device, such as your browsing preferences. Cookies help us remember your settings and enhance your experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of the website.

How we use your data?

We use your personal information to process orders, deliver digital products, and offer customer support. Your data allows us to fulfill any transactions and ensure smooth service delivery.

Your information is used primarily to provide our services, process transactions, and offer customer support. We may also use your data to personalize your experience and communicate important updates, promotional offers, or newsletters, which you can opt out of at any time. Ensuring the security of your data is a top priority for us, and we implement various security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse.

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers, such as payment processors or email marketing platforms, solely to help us deliver our services. These providers are obligated to keep your data secure and use it only for the purposes of assisting Aupulens. In certain situations, we may also disclose your data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Mode and place of processing the Data

Methods of processing

At Aupulens, we ensure that your personal data is processed securely and in compliance with applicable data protection laws. Data processing is carried out using computers and IT-enabled tools, following organizational procedures and policies that protect your privacy. We take all appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure of your personal information. Access to your data is limited to authorized personnel, including our employees, contractors, and third-party service providers, who are required to maintain confidentiality.

The data we collect is processed at Aupulens’ operational headquarters, as well as any other locations where our service providers operate. Depending on your location, data transfers may involve moving your information across borders. When this occurs, we ensure that the data is transferred in accordance with relevant data protection regulations, and adequate safeguards are in place to protect your personal information. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) requirements for international data transfers.

When we collect personal data about you

  • When you interact with us in person – through correspondence, by phone, by social media, or through our www.aupulens.com (“Site”).
  • When we get personal information from other legitimate sources, such as third-party data aggregators, Aupulens marketing partners, public sources, or social networks. We only use this data if you have given your consent to them to share your personal data with others.
  • We may collect personal data if it is considered to be of legitimate interest and if this interest is not overridden by your privacy interests. We make sure an assessment is made, with an established mutual interest between you and Aupulens.

Why we collect and use personal data

We collect and use personal data mainly to perform direct sales, direct marketing, and customer service. We also collect data about partners and persons seeking a job or working in our company.

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • Send you the marketing communications which you have requested. These may include information about our services, products, events, activities, and promotions of our partners. This communication is subscription-based and requires your consent.
  • Send you information about the services and products that you have purchased from us.
  • Perform direct sales activities in cases where legitimate and mutual interest is established.
  • Provide you with content and venue details on a webinar or event you signed up for.
  • Reply to a ‘Contact me’ or other web forms you have completed on our Site (e.g., to download an ebook).
  • Follow up on incoming requests (client support, emails, chats, or phone calls).
  • Perform contractual obligations such as invoices, reminders, and similar. The contract may be with Aupulens directly or with a Aupulens partner.
  • Notify you of any disruptions to our services.
  • Contact you to conduct surveys about your opinion on our services and products.
  • When we do a business deal or negotiate a business deal, involving the sale or transfer of all or a part of our business or assets. These deals can include any merger, financing, acquisition, or bankruptcy transaction or proceeding.
  • Process a job application.
  • To comply with laws.
  • To respond to lawful requests and legal process.
  • To protect the rights and property of Aupulens, our agents, customers, and others. Includes enforcing our agreements, policies, and terms of use.
  • In an emergency. Includes protecting the safety of our employees, our customers, or any person.

Type of personal data collected

We collect your email, full name, and company name. We may also collect feedback, comments, and questions received from you in service-related communication and activities, such as meetings, phone calls, chats, documents, and emails.

If you apply for a job at Aupulens, we collect the data you provide during the application process. Aupulens does not collect or process any particular categories of personal data, such as unique public identifiers or sensitive personal data.
